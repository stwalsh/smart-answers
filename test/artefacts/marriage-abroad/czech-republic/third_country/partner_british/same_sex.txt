Civil partnership in the Czech Republic

Same-sex relationships in the Czech Republic that are recognised as civil partnerships under British law are known as ’registrované partnerství’, or ‘registered partnership’.

Contact the relevant local authorities in the Czech Republic to find out about local laws, including what documents you’ll need.

You should also check the [travel advice for the Czech Republic](/foreign-travel-advice/czech-republic).
