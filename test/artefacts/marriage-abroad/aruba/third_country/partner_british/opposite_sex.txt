Marriage in Aruba

Aruba is one of the Dutch Caribbean islands.

Contact the relevant local authorities in Aruba to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.

^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for Aruba](/foreign-travel-advice/aruba) before making any plans.

There aren’t any British consular facilities to register a marriage or get legal documents to prove you’re eligible to marry in Aruba.

*[GRO]:General Register Office
*[FCO]:Foreign &amp; Commonwealth Office
