Marriage in Norway

Contact the relevant local authorities in Norway to find out about local marriage laws, including what documents you’ll need.

^You should [get legal advice](/government/collections/list-of-lawyers) and check the [travel advice for Norway](/foreign-travel-advice/norway) before making any plans.

##What you need to do

You’ll need to prove that you’re allowed to marry. There are 2 ways you can do this:

- [go to the UK and post notice with a UK registrar](/marriage-abroad/y/norway/uk/partner_other/opposite_sex)
- [go to Norway and swear an affidavit (written statement of facts) that you’re free to marry](/marriage-abroad/y/norway/ceremony_country/partner_other/opposite_sex)

*[CNI]:Certificate of no impediment
